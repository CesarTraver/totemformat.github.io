Pokémon TCG – Totem Format

A multiplayer format for Pokémon TCG focused on rotating objectives and thematic singleton decks built around a Totem Pokémon.

Read the Rules (v1.5)

What is Totem Format?

Multiplayer by Design

Designed for 3–6 players with mandatory rotating attack targets to avoid focus fire and keep the table balanced.

Gameplay

Objects can be used on opponents, attacks are enforced, and deals matter. Timing and negotiation are core skills.

Totem Pokémon

Each deck is defined by a powerful Totem Pokémon that sets color identity, strategy, and deck-building constraints.

Core Concepts

100-Card Singleton

No duplicate cards except Basic Energy. Pokémon may repeat only if they are mechanically different.

Prize Cards as Survival

You gain Prize cards when your Pokémon are defeated. Running out of Prizes eliminates you from the game.

Rotating Objectives

Each round assigns a new target via a die roll. You must attack them or pay a penalty.

Project Status

Totem Format is currently in version 1.5 and actively evolving. Community feedback and playtesting are encouraged.

Totem Format is a fan-made, non-commercial Pokémon TCG format.
